## Annual Billing Discount
All plans are 20% cheaper when billed annually.
- Starter annual: $278/yr (saves $70)
- Growth annual: $758/yr (saves $190)

## Enterprise Pricing
Enterprise pricing is custom and depends on seat count, ticket volume, and
required integrations. Contact sales@supportdeskpro.com for a quote.
Typical enterprise contracts range from $500-$5,000/month.

## Free Trial
All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
Trial accounts have Growth-level features. After the trial, you choose a plan or
the account is automatically downgraded to read-only mode (no new tickets accepted).

## Plan Upgrades and Downgrades
- Upgrades: take effect immediately; you are charged a prorated amount for the
  remainder of the current billing period.
- Downgrades: take effect at the start of the next billing period.
  Data is not deleted on downgrade, but features above your new plan limit
  become read-only until you upgrade again.
