# SupportDesk Pro — Return & Refund Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

## Software Subscription Refunds

SupportDesk Pro is a SaaS product. Because you gain immediate access to the software
upon purchase, the following terms apply:

### 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee
You may request a full refund within 14 calendar days of your initial purchase or
any renewal charge. No reason is required. To request a refund, email
support@supportdeskpro.com with subject line "Refund Request - [your account email]".

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Funds are returned to the original
payment method.

### After 14 Days
Refund requests after the 14-day window are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
Approved cases include:
- Documented service outages exceeding 48 cumulative hours in a billing period
- Billing errors (duplicate charges, incorrect plan charges)
- Inability to use the service due to a verified bug we cannot fix within 30 days

Denied cases include:
- Change of mind after the 14-day window
- Failure to cancel before a renewal date
- Partial month usage

### Annual Plans
Annual plans are refundable in full within 30 days of purchase.
After 30 days, a prorated refund is available for unused full months remaining.

---

## Add-Ons and One-Time Purchases

Add-ons (e.g., extra agent seats, premium integrations) purchased separately are
non-refundable after 7 days.
