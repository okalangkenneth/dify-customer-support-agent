# SupportDesk Pro — Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Billing

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
Go to app.supportdeskpro.com/login → click "Forgot password" → enter your email.
A reset link is sent within 2 minutes. Links expire after 24 hours.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
We do not accept prepaid cards or cryptocurrency.

**Q: When is my subscription renewed?**
Subscriptions renew on the same day each month. Example: if you signed up on
March 5th, your next charge is April 5th. You receive a reminder email 3 days before.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
Yes. We offer a full refund within 14 days of any charge, no questions asked.
After 14 days, refunds are considered case-by-case. Contact support@supportdeskpro.com.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the
end of your current billing period. You keep access until then.

## Features & Usage

**Q: How many agents can I add to my account?**
- Starter plan: up to 3 agents
- Growth plan: up to 15 agents
- Enterprise plan: unlimited agents

**Q: Does SupportDesk Pro integrate with Slack?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack. You need a Slack workspace admin token.
Notifications for new tickets, assignments, and SLA breaches are supported.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
iOS and Android apps are available. Search "SupportDesk Pro" in the App Store or
Google Play. The mobile app supports viewing and replying to tickets but not
creating automations or editing macros.

**Q: What is the SLA response time?**
- Starter: best effort (no SLA guarantee)
- Growth: first response within 8 business hours
- Enterprise: first response within 2 business hours, 99.9% uptime SLA

**Q: Can I import tickets from Zendesk or Intercom?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Import Data → select your source. Supported formats:
Zendesk CSV export, Intercom JSON export. Migration takes 1-4 hours depending on volume.

## Technical & Security

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
We are SOC 2 Type II certified. Annual penetration tests are conducted by a third party.

**Q: Where are your servers located?**
Primary: US-East (Virginia). Backup: EU-West (Ireland).
Enterprise customers can request EU-only data residency.

**Q: Do you offer SSO?**
Yes, on Growth and Enterprise plans. We support SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0.
Setup guide: docs.supportdeskpro.com/sso-setup

**Q: What is your uptime history?**
We maintain a public status page at status.supportdeskpro.com.
Our 12-month rolling uptime is 99.94%.
